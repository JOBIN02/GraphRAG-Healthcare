SUBJECT: Macrovascular Complications
STATISTIC: CVD is the #1 cause of death in people with diabetes.

1. THE MECHANISM
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
* **Glycation:** Sugar sticks to LDL cholesterol, making it smaller and denser ("small dense LDL").
* **Oxidation:** This sugar-coated LDL easily penetrates artery walls.
* **Inflammation:** High sugar damages the endothelial lining of blood vessels, creating rough spots where plaque accumulates.
* **Result:** Accelerated Atherosclerosis (hardening of arteries).

2. ABCs OF HEART PROTECTION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
* **A1c:** Manage blood sugar.
* **Blood Pressure:** Target <130/80 mmHg.
* **Cholesterol:** Most diabetics over age 40 are recommended a Statin therapy regardless of cholesterol levels.
* **Smoking:** Cessation is the single most effective intervention.
